Haircutting Safety Rules
  • Keep the clients face free from hair clippings.
  • Never cut beyond your safety zone of your fingers.
  • If you drop a comb or pair of scissors on the floor, clean the. and sanitize – or – get a new pair before you continue cutting.
  • Ensure your own posture and position while working.
